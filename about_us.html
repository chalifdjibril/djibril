About Us

Welcome to DC Internet, your number one source for internet. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best of network, with a focus on speed, customer service, and uniqueness. Founded in 2024 by Djibril Chalif, DCInternet has come a long way from its beginnings in RUHENGERI. When Djibril Chalif first started out, his passion for connect people to the world through internet drove him to creating an internet company, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming internet connectivity. We now serve customers all over the country, and are thrilled to be a part of the new connected WORLD. We hope you enjoy our service as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
